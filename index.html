About Us

OUR MOTTO IS THE QUALITY OF WORKS.

We are a company with tradition in mount and repair of roofs for over 20 years. We do any work in terms of roofing and civil constructions. We install roofs as well as all systems of covering.
The prices are accessible by bringing the material from the factory directly to the work. We offer you EXTENDED WARRANTY!



We are experts in Roofing and Covering Systems installation, and sale with warranty.
We mount different kind of tiles: metal, lindab, ceramic, galvanized, shingle. We also perform carpentry for roofing, attics and roof windows.



We offer you the installation of the resistance structure(carpentry) for your home in the most professional way with: rafters, clamps, skylights etc. We perform interior and exterior insulation works, as well as professional finishes!
We offer you a solid and good quality construction in the shortest time possible, without any hidden problems or misunderstandings with our builders.



We perform proffesional fitting at the best prices on the market. We perform carpentry and tinkering with our materials or the client's:
- Renovation and replacement of the old roof
- Execution of new roofs regardless of type and complexity
- Installation of rain systems etc.
